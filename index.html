Kyle Martin

Game Developer & Lifelong Learner

Hello!


My name is Kyle Martin and I love to make games.

I love to code. I love art and stories. I love music. And I believe these passions all go hand in hand. My greatest ambitions lie at the intersection of art and technology. For me, there is no better medium for artistic and technical expression than video games.

At my core, I am a self starter that believes learning is best accomplished through creating, and learning game development was one of the best decisions I've ever made. Through making my first two games and learning from other creators, I have developed a passion for all facets of video game development and design: programming, 3D-modeling, music composition, storytelling, lighting and rendering, and beyond. I look forward to future projects, collaborations, and opportunities in the world of games!
